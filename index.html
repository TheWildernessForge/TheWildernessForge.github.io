Care Guide

Keeping your terrarium happy

Good news: our terrariums are designed to be low-maintenance. Here's everything you need to know.

☀️

Light

Place in bright, indirect light. A north-facing windowsill or 60 cm from a sunny window is ideal. Avoid harsh direct sun which can scorch leaves.

💧

Watering

Open terrariums: water lightly once a week. Closed terrariums: mist once a month or when the glass looks completely clear of condensation.

🌡️

Temperature

Keep between 15–25°C. Avoid placing near radiators, air vents, or draughty windows. Room temperature is usually perfect.

✂️

Trimming

If plants grow too large, trim carefully with small scissors. Remove any yellowed leaves promptly to keep the ecosystem healthy.
