Care Guide

Keep your terrarium happy

Simple care instructions reduce customer questions and make your products feel more premium.

☀️ Light

Keep terrariums in bright indirect light. Avoid harsh direct sun, especially for closed jars.

💧 Water

Open moss terrariums need light watering weekly. Closed terrariums usually only need occasional misting.

🌡️ Temperature

Room temperature is ideal. Avoid radiators, cold windows, and strong drafts.

✂️ Trimming

Trim overgrown plants and remove yellow leaves to keep the ecosystem clean and balanced.
